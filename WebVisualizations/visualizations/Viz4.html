Wind Speed




We found that the wind speed tends to decrease as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). This relationship is strong enough to be considered significant. Also, interestingly enough, the northern hemisphere tends to have higher wind speeds this time of year than the southern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
